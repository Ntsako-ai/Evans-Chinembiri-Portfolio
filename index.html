Evans W. K. Chinembiri

Private Sector Development Specialist • Investment Climate Advisor • Economic Strategist

I help governments, development partners, and private sector leaders unlock markets, mobilise investment, and build competitive, resilient economies across Africa.

Bio

I’ve spent my career at the intersection of markets, policy, and development. I bring a 360-degree view of how policy, investment, and enterprise growth come together.

I’ve led multi-country programmes worth over US$100M and chaired regional policy clusters that influence entire sectors.

Colleagues describe me as a bridge-builder: someone who can speak the language of economists, investors, policymakers, and entrepreneurs.

Let’s Work Together: If you’re seeking to unlock markets or drive inclusive growth in Southern Africa, I’d be glad to connect.
